Hello! I'm Ayush.

I'm currently a Senior Associate at JPMorgan Chase, contributing to the Securitized Products Group with a focus on Non-Agency MBS modeling. I graduated with a Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.

I'm proficient in machine learning, deep learning, and mathematics, leveraging these skills to address complex financial modeling challenges. My work involves predictive modeling and large language models to enhance quantitative analysis in the financial sector.

Previously, I've contributed to impactful projects including COVID-19 response systems and autonomous vehicle research, highlighting my robust analytical and problem-solving capabilities. I led the software development for Team AUV-IITK's autonomous underwater vehicle, achieving first runner-up at the national level AUV competition.

I'm passionate about applying cutting-edge technology to solve real-world problems, whether in finance, robotics, or environmental conservation through machine learning.
